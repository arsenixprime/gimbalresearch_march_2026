## Key Gaps and Limitations

- **Defense/military products** (Aero Sentinel, Controp, Edge Autonomy, NextVision, Trillium, UkrSpecSystems) rarely publish pricing or detailed specifications publicly. Quotes must be requested directly from manufacturers.
- **Weight data** is missing for several products. Some listed weights are gross/shipping weights that include packaging, which may overstate the actual gimbal weight. Notable examples: Skydroid C20 (1000g may be gross weight), Foxtech Seeker series (shipping weights only).
- **OEM/ODM manufacturers** (Viewsheen, Xingkai) do not publicly list prices, making cost evaluation impossible without direct inquiry.
- **Chinese-origin products** dominate the market but may face NDAA compliance restrictions for US government procurement. DJI products are explicitly not NDAA compliant.
- **NDAA-compliant options** are limited: Gremsy (Vietnam), Trillium Engineering (USA), and potentially UXV Technologies (Denmark) are the primary non-Chinese sources with qualifying or near-qualifying products.
- **Proprietary ecosystems**: DJI Zenmuse products require DJI Matrice drones (Skyport interface). Many other products use open protocols (MAVLink, S.Bus, PWM) for broader compatibility.
- **Price variability**: Enterprise gimbal prices can vary significantly by retailer and configuration. Some products have sale prices substantially below MSRP.
- **Near-miss products** often fail on weight alone -- several excellent gimbals weigh 1000-1100g, suggesting the 1000g threshold may exclude otherwise strong candidates.
