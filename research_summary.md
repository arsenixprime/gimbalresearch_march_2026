## Market Gaps Identified

1. **No non-Chinese commercial options under $10k:** All qualifying products are from Chinese or Vietnamese manufacturers. Western buyers seeking non-Chinese supply chains have no commercially available options.

2. **Gap between 10x and 20x zoom:** There are no qualifying products with 12-19x optical zoom in the commercial space. The jump from 10x budget gimbals to 20x+ mid-range is abrupt.

3. **4K + high zoom is rare:** Only DJI (H30 series at 34x) and SIYI (ZR30 at 30x) offer 4K resolution with 30x+ optical zoom. Most high-zoom gimbals are limited to 1080P.

4. **Lightweight high-zoom gap:** No product under 600g offers more than 10x optical zoom. High zoom requires heavier optics.

5. **Price gap at 30x+:** The 30x+ zoom segment splits sharply between budget Chinese options ($1,177-$2,000) and premium DJI/Foxtech options ($3,850-$7,899). Little middle ground exists.

6. **AI tracking limited to premium:** AI object tracking/detection is only available on higher-priced models (Foxtech SEEKER series, SIYI ZR30, Viewpro A10T Pro).
