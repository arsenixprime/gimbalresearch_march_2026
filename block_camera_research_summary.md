## Key Gaps and Limitations

1. **Weight data is sparse:** Only 39.1% of products have published weight. Many Chinese OEM manufacturers (Savgood, Viewsheen, YXF) do not publish module weight on their websites, requiring direct sales contact.

2. **Price data is very limited:** Only 15.9% of products have pricing. Most industrial/OEM block cameras require contacting the manufacturer for quotes. Available prices come from Alibaba/distributor listings and may not reflect current or volume pricing.

3. **Stabilization data is inconsistent:** Many products list stabilization as "unknown" because manufacturers do not prominently feature this specification. Electronic defog is sometimes conflated with EIS in product descriptions.

4. **Discontinued products included:** Some products (Sony FCB-EV7520, FCB-ER8550, Dahua CA-HZ2030T, Hikvision DS-2ZMN2007) are discontinued but included for reference as they may still be available on the secondary market.

5. **OEM/white-label overlap:** Some products from different brands may use the same optical/sensor assemblies. For example, iShot XBlock cameras use KT&C modules, and several Chinese manufacturers likely share common lens assemblies (e.g., the 30x 4.7-141mm lens appears across Savgood, Viewsheen, and others).

6. **Zoom criteria interpretation:** The "10x-40x" criteria is applied to optical zoom only. Some products with lower optical zoom offer higher effective zoom through digital or "fusion" zoom techniques.

7. **Weight criteria interpretation:** Products are marked as qualifying if their known weight is under 1000g OR if weight is unknown but all other criteria are met (zoom range). Products where weight is unknown but suspected to exceed 1000g are marked as near-miss.

8. **Physical dimensions are inconsistently reported:** Dimension format varies between manufacturers (LxWxH, HxWxD, or only partial dimensions). Direct comparison requires careful interpretation.

9. **Country of origin for Skoopia:** Listed as Germany based on company headquarters, though optical components may be sourced from Asia.

10. **Sensor specifications:** Several manufacturers use similar Sony sensors (IMX327, IMX385, IMX462, IMX334) across different camera modules, making sensor-level comparison possible even when other specs differ.
