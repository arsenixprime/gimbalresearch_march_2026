# Drone Optical Zoom Gimbal Research

Research database of drone-mountable camera gimbals with integrated optical zoom capability.

**Criteria:** 10x+ optical zoom, under 1kg, under $10,000 USD, daylight/visible spectrum.

**Generated:** 2026-03-14

## Key Results

- [qualifying_products.md](qualifying_products.md) - 28 products meeting all criteria
- [near_misses.md](near_misses.md) - 14 products that almost qualify
- [research_summary.md](research_summary.md) - Full market analysis, trends, and gaps
