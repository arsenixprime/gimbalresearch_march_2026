# Drone Optical Zoom Gimbal Research

Research database of drone-mountable camera gimbals with integrated optical zoom capability.

**Criteria:** 10x+ optical zoom, under 1kg, under $10,000 USD, daylight/visible spectrum.

**Generated:** 2026-03-15

- **65 products** researched from **23 manufacturers**
- **37 qualifying products** meeting all criteria
- **17 near-miss products** (close but fail one criterion)
- Price range of qualifying: $379 - $9,809
- Weight range of qualifying: 180g - 1000g
